Ham And Cheese Sandwich

Description

A ham and cheese sandwich is a sandwich compound by some ham, cheese and eventually certain addings like mayonaisse, ketchup or mustard. It can be toasted too!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Some cheese slices
  • Some ham slices
  • Mayonaisse

Steps

  1. Put mayonaisse on one side of the first bread slice
  2. Put slices of ham and cheese on one side of the second bread piece.
  3. Put the side with the mayonaisse of the first bread slice on the ham and cheese side of the second bread piece.
  4. Optionally: You can toast the sandwich with some butter on a pan, flipping on both sides.
  5. Enjoy!
