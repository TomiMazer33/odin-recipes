Spaghetti With Meatballs

Description

Spaghetti and meatballs is an Italian and Italian-American dish consisting of spaghetti, tomato sauce and meatballs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Meatballs

Steps

  1. Pour some sauce over the spaghetti
  2. Put meatballs on the previous mix
  3. Heat it up on the microwave
  4. Enjoy!! (:
